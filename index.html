Intro

Throughout my Data Analytics journey at Thinkful, I learned how to extract and present actionable insights that deliver business value from common data sources like Excel, CSV files, and SQL databases. I am proficient in Excel, PowerPoint, PostgreSQL and PG Admin, Tableau, and Python for data analysis. As much as I am into data wrangling and manipulation, it is the data analysis, data visualization, and storytelling with data that I enjoy the most. I like organizing data and finding relationships between the variables, as well as thinking critically and logically about the problems the data might present. I spend my time finding trends and patterns to uncover new opportunities in data. Check out my work.
